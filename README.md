### About The Project

The Situation - Played the role of a Data Analyst for healthcare analytics consulting firm that helps hospitals use data to improve patient care and reduce costs

Given Task - To prepare Massachusetts General Hospital's annual performance report and analyze patient encounters, costs, coverage and behavior trends to support planning and improve care and operations

1st Objective - Encounters Overview

- Trends in encounter volume, types & lengths

2nd Objective - Cost & Coverage Insights

- Analyzed payer coverage, top procedures by frequency and cost, and average claim costs by payer

3rd Objective - Patient Behavior analysis

- Analyzed patient behavior by tracking quarterly admissions and 30-day readmissions
